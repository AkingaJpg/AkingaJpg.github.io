About

The Hand
behind the work

I'm an illustrator and communication designer whose work sits at the intersection of narrative imagery, type, and cultural memory. My practice draws on Nordic mythology, folklore, and the visual language of natural history — treating darkness not as absence, but as a medium in itself. A member of IO — Illustrationen Organisation e.V.
